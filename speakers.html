Dr. Priyanka Anoshiri

Dr. Priyanka Anoshiri is a pastor, medical doctor, and clinical psychologist dedicated to empowering women to embrace their God-given identity and purpose. As Convener of the Kingdom Women Conference, she creates transformative spaces where women experience healing, mentorship, and spiritual growth. With a holistic approach that integrates faith, mental wellness, and practical guidance, Dr. Priyanka inspires women to live boldly, break limiting mindsets, and impact their families, churches, and communities for the Kingdom of God.

Dr. Serah Mwangi

Dr. Serah Mwangi is an accomplished Emergency Medicine Specialist and Flight Physician based in Poland, with over a decade of expertise in trauma care, critical care, and emergency response. She serves with the Polish Helicopter Emergency Medical Services, the country’s single MEDVAC hub, and a county hospital, and has participated in high-impact international humanitarian missions, including COVID-19 response in Ethiopia and crisis interventions at the Polish–Belarusian border. Passionate about patient-centered care and leadership, Dr. Mwangi empowers women to rise with purpose, resilience, and faith in both their personal and professional lives.

Samantha Olivia Bantu

Samantha Olivia Bantu is a dynamic educator and community builder dedicated to empowering African educators and fostering cross-cultural collaboration in Europe. As founder of the African Teachers Network Poland, she drives initiatives in professional development, inclusion, and Pan-African advocacy, building bridges between Africa and Europe through education, cultural exchange, and sustainable partnerships.

Salome Rumbi Chamboko

Salome Rumbi Chamboko is a financial wellbeing expert, speaker, and author of Winning the Money Game: Lessons You Can’t Afford to Ignore. Combining professional expertise, lived experience, and Christian faith, she empowers individuals and business owners to manage money with clarity, confidence, and purpose. Through coaching, workshops, and community initiatives, Salome helps people build financial confidence, grow sustainable income, and make tax-efficient decisions without overwhelm, inspiring intentional, values-driven living.
